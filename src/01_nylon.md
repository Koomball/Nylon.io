**Install Package** <br>
Command: `nyl install` <br>
Example: `nyl i discord.nyl` <br>
use @ after package name to define specific version. <br>
Example: `nyl i discord.nyl@v0.1` <br>

**Run startScript.nyl** <br>
Command: `nyl startScript`
